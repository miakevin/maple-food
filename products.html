Mycotoxin Binders

The prevention of mycotoxin formation is essential since there are few ways to completely overcome problems once mycotoxins are present.

Mycosal’s is synergistic combination of Alluminum Silicate and other silicates, organic acid, their salts and surfactants Mycosal’s action mechanism is to adsorb and keep tied mycotoxins in the gastrointestinal tract so they can be excreted in the feces.

The toxicity is so reduced or eliminated , improving nutrients absorption Mycosal support is a combination of positive and negative electric charges. In this way is possible to adsorb a wide range of mycotoxins because they can be positive or negative electric charged.

Energising

The product aim is the treatment and prevention of ketosis, a metabolic syndrome of a serious deficiency of energetic credits in comparison with debits and which interests the 4% -12% of cattle during the first 60 days of lactation.

Malnutrition: the cow doesn’t ingest food in right quantities because of elevated summer temperature. Primary ketosis: the cow assumes ketogenic food (ex. Some bad insulated).

Spontaneous primary ketosis: the cowseems to be correctly rationed, but in reality there is a lack of balance between glucogenetic and lipogenetic substance.

Organic Trace Elements

There are three kinds of different chelated trace minerals: with Hydrolyzed Soya, (dry form) Glycine (dry form) and Methionine, (dry form). The trace mineral chelates are better absorbed in the gut, because other ingredients present in the feed above all salts don’t interfere with their absorption, so you can be sure that a proper amount of trace mineral is really available, and not present in faeces, it is important to avoid environmental problems and to respect law limits.
